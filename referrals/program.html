The Rules

How Referrals Pay Out

01
Use your code
Every team member gets an ambassador code. The couple mentions your code, signs the contract, and the referral is credited to you.
02
Paid only when they book
A booked wedding means signed contract and retainer received. Inquiries, leads, and consults do not trigger payment. Bookings do.
03
Same payout, any role
It does not matter whether you end up shooting the wedding as lead, as second, or not at all. The referral payout is the same.
Step by Step

From Conversation to Payout

  1. Step 01
    Share your ambassador code with a couple Mention us in a real moment. A friend just got engaged, a couple at a venue is asking around, a DM rolls in. Send them our way and pass along your code.
  2. Step 02
    They inquire and book The couple uses your code on their inquiry form, books a consult, and signs a contract for one of the three packages.
  3. Step 03
    Referral logged on contract signing Once the contract is signed and the retainer hits, your referral is locked in at the tier matching their package.
  4. Step 04
    You get paid Referral bonus is paid out within the next pay cycle. Zelle, Venmo, or check (your call). Tracked on your team dashboard so you always know what is pending.
